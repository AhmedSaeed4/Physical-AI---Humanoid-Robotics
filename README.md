# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Book

This repository contains the educational resource "Embodied Intelligence: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics" - a comprehensive guidebook that bridges modern generative AI with hard robotics using the NVIDIA ecosystem.

## Structure

- `frontend/` - Docusaurus-based documentation site with book content in `docs/`
- `backend/` - Supporting services
- `physical_ai_ws/` - ROS 2 workspace with code examples (created during setup)

## Book Content

The book is organized into 12 chapters across 6 parts:

### Part I: Infrastructure
- Chapter 1: Hardware & OS Configuration
- Chapter 2: The Edge Ecosystem

### Part II: ROS
- Chapter 3: ROS 2 Architecture
- Chapter 4: The Body (URDF & Kinematics)

### Part III: Simulation
- Chapter 5: Physics in Gazebo
- Chapter 6: Photorealism with NVIDIA Isaac Sim

### Part IV: Perception
- Chapter 7: Sensors & VSLAM
- Chapter 8: Navigation

### Part V: AI Integration
- Chapter 9: The Ears (Voice Pipeline)
- Chapter 10: The Brain (LLM Action Planning)
- Chapter 11: Vision-Language-Action (VLA)

### Part VI: Capstone
- Chapter 12: The Autonomous Humanoid

## Setup

For development environment setup, follow the instructions in Chapter 1 of the book or see the quickstart guide.
